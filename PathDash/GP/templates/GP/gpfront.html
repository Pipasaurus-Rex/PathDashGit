Welcome to The Pathology GP Requesting Dashboard!

This Dashboard is being developed to help visualise pathology requesting data to enable a more streamlined pathology service.
We hope that use of this dashboard will help to reduce un-necessary pathology requesting and increase efficiency.
This Dashboard is also being used for the dashboard creator Pip Croxford's MSc Project in Health Informatics.
For any queries relating to this dashboard please contact her at pip.croxford@heartofengland.nhs.uk .
